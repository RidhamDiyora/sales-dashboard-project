# Sales Trend Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview

This project presents an **interactive sales analytics dashboard built using Tableau** to analyze business performance across different regions and product categories. The dashboard highlights key metrics such as total sales, profit, and profit margin while providing insights into sales trends and profitability. 

---

## Key Metrics

The dashboard tracks the following KPIs:

* **Total Sales:** $272,419
* **Total Profit:** $50,834
* **Profit Margin:** 12.87%

These KPIs provide a quick overview of overall business performance. 

---

## Dashboard Features

### Sales Trend

A time-series chart showing how sales change over time, helping identify growth patterns and fluctuations in revenue. 

### Category Sales

A bar chart comparing sales performance across product categories such as Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology. 

### Region Profit

A visualization of profit distribution across regions including Central, East, South, and West. 

### Sales vs Profit Analysis

A scatter plot used to analyze the relationship between sales and profit across product segments, helping identify high-performing and low-margin products. 

---

## Tools Used

* **Tableau Public**
* Data Visualization

## Key Insights

* Technology category generates the highest sales among product categories.
* Some products have high sales but relatively lower profit.
* Regional profit distribution varies significantly across different regions.
